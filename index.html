Women Of Wonder

Seeking to bridge the gender gap in the tech industry!


We want to provide younger girls with a community of powerful role models in the tech industry that they can look to for inspiration and learn about their work and opportunities. Through this, girls - from any background, culture, identity, socioeconomic status - can see that they too belong in the groundbreaking field of technology and can be successful at it.

By clicking the "role models" button, you can scroll through our website to find powerful women in technology and learn about their work, opportunities, and become inspired.
